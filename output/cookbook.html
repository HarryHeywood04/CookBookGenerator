Brookie

Servings: Lots Time: 60 minutes Ingredients:
200g - Dark chocolate
250g - Butter
4 - Eggs
215g - Sugar
220g - Flour
Extras:
Chocolate chips
 Steps:
1. Brownie: Melt chocolate and 150g butter and combine, then add 3 eggs, 150g sugar and 90g flour.
2. Cookie: Melt 100g butter and mix 165g sugar then add 1 egg and 230g flour then add chocolate chips.
3. Put brownie in tin then cook for 10 mins at 160°C then add cookie dough and cook for another 20-30 mins at 180°C.

Chicken Jalfrezi

Servings: 4 Time: 60 minutes Ingredients:
2 tbsp - Vegetable oil
1 - Onion
2 - Garlic cloves
1 tsp - Chilli powder
3 tsp - Ground tumeric
750g - Chicken thigh
400g - Chopped tomatoes
2 tsp - Ground ginger
3 tsp - Ground cumin
3vtsp - Ground coriander
2 tbsp - Melted butter
Extras:
Lemon juice
Coriander
Red chilies
Lime
Rice
 Steps:
1. Dice chicken then chop onion and crush garlic.
2. Heat the oil in a pan and fry onion and garlic over a medium heat till softened. Mix the chilli powder, turmeric and a pinch of salt together in a bowl then add the chicken pieces and mix well to coat, add chicken to pan and fry for 10-15 mins until golden brown.
3. Stir the tomatoes, ginger, cumin and coriander into the pan then reduce heat until the mixture is simmering, cook for 20-30 mins stirring occasionally and adding a tablespoon of water if the mixture seems too dry. Meanwhile cook the rice. Then stir in the butter to the chicken and add lemon juice to taste.
4. Serve and garnish with lime wedges, red chillies and coriander.

Chicken Tikka Masala

Servings: 4 Time: 50 minutes Ingredients:
1 cup/240g - Full-fat yogurt
2 tsp - Kosher salt
2.25 tbsp/20g - Garam masala
2 tsp/6g - Paprika
5 - Garlic cloves (finely chopped)
2x 2 inch knobs - Ginger (grated)
900g - Chicken thighs (diced)
1 - Onion (thinly sliced)
1 tsp/3g - Cayenne powder
425g - Can of crushed tomatoes
1 cup - Heavy cream
Extras:
Sugar
Coriander
Rice
 Steps:
1. In a medium-sized bowl, add yogurt, salt, 1.25tsp/11g garam masala, 1 tsp/3g paprika, garlic, and 1 of the knobs of ginger; whisk to combine. Next, add chicken to your bowl, toss to coat thoroughly, and marinate for 10 minutes or overnight in the fridge.
2. Set a pan over medium-high heat, and add enough oil to coat the bottom of the pan. Once hot and nearly smoking, add all your chicken in batches and sear for 2 - 3 minutes per side until nicely browned on all edges.
3. Remove chicken, add additional oil if needed, and lower the heat to medium. Add onion, ginger, and garlic. Season lightly with salt, and saute for 3 - 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until softened. Next, add paprika, cayenne, garam masala, and sugar; saute until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
4. Add crushed tomatoes, stir and reduce for 5 minutes. Optionally, pour into a blender and blend on high speed until as smooth as possible. Place back in the pot, reduce the heat to low, add all your chicken, and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 10 minutes or until the chicken is thoroughly cooked and the sauce is reduced.
5. Stir in heavy cream and reduce for 7 - 8 more minutes or until the sauce is thick and velvety—adjust salt levels if necessary. Serve immediately with rice. Optionally garnish with coriander.

Penne al arrabbiata

Servings: 2 Time: 25 minutes Ingredients:
250g - Penne pasta
400g - Tinned tomatoes
4 - Garlic cloves
2 tsp - Chilli flakes
Extras:
Parmesan
Parsley
Olive Oil
 Steps:
1. Chop parsley stems off and chop leaves, crush 2 cloves of garlic and chop the other 2.
2. Pour enough olive oil to coat bottom of pan and add the chopped garlic and parsley stems, cook on low heat for a few minutes
3. Remove parsley and garlic and then add the minced garlic and the chilli flakes, then cook for about 2 mins till garlic is nice and toasted.
4. Add the tomatoes and crush them up, add pinch of salt and a little bit of sugar, cook for about 10-15 mins.
5. Heavily salt pasta water and cook penne then remove pasta from water and add to sauce along with some pasta water, when ready add a load of parsley and mix then serve with parmesan

Spaghetti Pomodoro

Servings: 2-4 Time: 45 minutes Ingredients:
500g - Tomatoes
5 - Garlic Cloves
1 - Onion
1 Handful - Basil
2 Tbsp - Tomato Paste
Extras:
Parmesan
Olive Oil
Spaghetti
 Steps:
1. Pre-heat oven to 180, halve the tomatoes and peel garlic. Then toss on baking tray and cover in salt, pepper and olive oil. Put in to roast for 35 minutes.
2. Dice onion and fry in olive oil till soft and brown, then add squeeze of tomato paste and cook for a few minutes to get rid of bitter taste.
3. Heavily salt pasta water and add spaghetti, then cook for 12 minutes.
4. Add onion, tomatoes, garlic and juices to blender. Blend till smooth then add a handful of basil and blend till chopped.
5. Pour sauce into pan and add salt and pepper to taste. Then simmer for a few minutes before introducing spaghetti, add some pasta water to enhance flavour. Add parmesan and a little more pasta water.
 JAPANESE

Bonito Donburi

Servings: 1 Time: 20 minutes Ingredients:
150g - Protein
1 tsp - Ginger
1 - Onion
2 - Spring onions
1 tbsp - Bonito powder
1 - Egg
100g - White rice
Extras:
Soy sauce
 Steps:
1. Add rice to pan and then add 3/4 cup of water, bring to boil then reduce to simmer, cover with lid, cook for 12 mins then rest for 10 mins.
2. Chop onions and stir fry the protein, then add onions and add water till almost fully submerged. Add bonito powder and soy sauce so it’s a little saltier than desired.
3. Beat egg and add to pan and cook till done.
4. Serve rice and then donburi on top.

Shoyu Ramen (quick)

Servings: 1 Time: 30 minutes Ingredients:
1 tsp - Sesame oil
1 tsp - Ginger
1 - Garlic clove
2 cups - Chicken stock
1 cup - Kombu dashi soup stock
3 tbsps - Soy sauce
1 tsp - Sugar
1 tsp - Salt
Extras:
Ramen noodles
Spring onion
Nori
Protein
Egg
Bamboo shoots
Bean sprouts
Sake
 Steps:
1. Crush garlic, chop ginger, prepare chicken and dashi soup stock. (Prepare Optionals) Start cooking protein to go with meal.
2. Heat sesame oil in a deep pan over medium heat, Sauté the ginger and garlic in pan for about a minute, then lower heat and add both stocks to the pan, bring to a boil.
3. Add the soy sauce, sugar, salt, (sake), and bring to a boil again. Meanwhile boil noodles following package instructions. Once noodles are done boil egg.
4. Strain soup into bowls then add noodles and toppings.

Red curry

Author: Harry Heywood
Servings: 1 Time: 30 minutes Ingredients:
50g - Red curry paste
200g - Chicken
360ml - Coconut milk
100g - Rice
Extras:
Coriander
Thai Basil
Vegetables
 Steps:
1. Finely chop the coriander and veg. Dice chicken.
2. Stir fry the curry paste in oil and then add 1cup of coconut milk, stir through then add meat and simmer. Meanwhile, add rice to a pan with 3/4 cup of water and bring to boil, then reduce to simmer and cover for 12 minutes then rest for 10 minutes.
3. Add another 1/2 cup of coconut milk with some water if necessary, then add coriander and veg. Allow the curry to simmer for 12 mins when water has reduced, add 1 1/2 tsp sugar and if necessary add some cornstarch mixed with water and simmer for a few more minutes.
