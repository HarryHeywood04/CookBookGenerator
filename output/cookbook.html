Chicken Tikka Masala


Servings: 4
Time: 50 minutes
Ingredients:
1 cup/240g - Full-fat yogurt
2 tsp - Kosher salt
2.25 tbsp/20g - Garam masala
2 tsp/6g - Paprika
5 - Garlic cloves (finely chopped)
2x 2 inch knobs - Ginger (grated)
900g - Chicken thighs (diced)
1 - Onion (thinly sliced)
1 tsp/3g - Cayenne powder
425g - Can of crushed tomatoes
1 cup - Heavy cream
Extras:
Sugar
Coriander
Rice
 Steps:
1. In a medium-sized bowl, add yogurt, salt, 1.25tsp/11g garam masala, 1 tsp/3g paprika, garlic, and 1 of the knobs of ginger; whisk to combine. Next, add chicken to your bowl, toss to coat thoroughly, and marinate for 10 minutes or overnight in the fridge.
2. Set a pan over medium-high heat, and add enough oil to coat the bottom of the pan. Once hot and nearly smoking, add all your chicken in batches and sear for 2 - 3 minutes per side until nicely browned on all edges.
3. Remove chicken, add additional oil if needed, and lower the heat to medium. Add onion, ginger, and garlic. Season lightly with salt, and saute for 3 - 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until softened. Next, add paprika, cayenne, garam masala, and sugar; saute until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
4. Add crushed tomatoes, stir and reduce for 5 minutes. Optionally, pour into a blender and blend on high speed until as smooth as possible. Place back in the pot, reduce the heat to low, add all your chicken, and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 10 minutes or until the chicken is thoroughly cooked and the sauce is reduced.

Spaghetti Pomodoro


Servings: 2-4
Time: 45 minutes
Ingredients:
500g - Tomatoes
5 - Garlic Cloves
1 - Onion
1 Handful - Basil
2 Tbsp - Tomato Paste
Extras:
Parmesan
Olive Oil
Spaghetti
 Steps:
1. Pre-heat oven to 180, halve the tomatoes and peel garlic. Then toss on baking tray and cover in salt, pepper and olive oil. Put in to roast for 35 minutes.
2. Dice onion and fry in olive oil till soft and brown, then add squeeze of tomato paste and cook for a few minutes to get rid of bitter taste.
3. Heavily salt pasta water and add spaghetti, then cook for 12 minutes.
4. Add onion, tomatoes, garlic and juices to blender. Blend till smooth then add a handful of basil and blend till chopped.
5. Pour sauce into pan and add salt and pepper to taste. Then simmer for a few minutes before introducing spaghetti, add some pasta water to enhance flavour. Add parmesan and a little more pasta water.
